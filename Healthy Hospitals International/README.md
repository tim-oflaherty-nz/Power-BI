Healthy Hospitals International runs hospitals around the world, and partners with external 24 hour Urgent Care Centres where there is a Healthy Hospitals International 24 hour Emergency Department operating, to handle overflow patients who are triaged as Triage Levels 4 and 5.

This dashboard analyses Healthy Hospitals International Emergency Department data for the period 1 July 2024 to 1 July 2025 inclusive, and the number of referrals to partner facilities, as well as discharges from the Emergency Department to other departments within a HHI Hospital

Parameter/Slicer Inputs
The following parameters are in effect:

Overnight Shift Start and Overnight Shift End allows the user to specify which hours constitute Overnight shifts. Other hours are, by default, allocated as being Daytime shifts.
Time Period Selected allows the user to select if they wish to see the results from Daytime, overnight, or both (All) shifts.
Age Range allows for a filter based on the age ranges in the dropdown. These are:
00 to 05 Years old.
06 to 13 Years old.
14 to 17 Years old.
18 to 64 Years old.
65 Years and Older.
Age Bands allow for a more narrowed focus on the age range, the dropdown options are 00 to 04 Years old, 05 to 09 Years old and so forth.

Triage Level. The Triage Levels are 
Triage Level 1 (immediate, life-threatening).
Triage Level 2 (imminently life-threatening, 10-minute wait time), 
Triage Level 3 (potentially life-threatening, 30-minute wait time), 
Triage Level 4 (potentially serious, 60-minute wait time), and 
Triage Level 5 (non-urgent, can wait up to two hours).
